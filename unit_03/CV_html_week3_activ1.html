Work History

  1. Supervillain: Gru was a notorious supervillain who wanted to be the world's greatest villain. He stole things, including Queen Elizabeth II's crown and the Zodiac Stone.
  2. Jelly manufacturer: Gru also worked as an artisanal jelly manufacturer.
  3. AVL agent: Gru is now a secret agent for the AVL, an organization that fights evil.

Skills

  • Leadership: Gru is a natural leader with charisma and high-level intelligence.
  • Weapon proficiency: Gru is skilled with weapons like the Freeze Ray, Shrink Ray, and Fart Gun.
  • Minions: Gru leads an army of Minions.
